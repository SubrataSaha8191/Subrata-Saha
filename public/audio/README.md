# Audio Files

## Required Sounds

### sip.mp3

A short sipping sound effect for the AboutRoom coffee interaction.

You can:

1. Download a free sipping sound from sites like:
   - freesound.org
   - pixabay.com/sound-effects
   - zapsplat.com

2. Or record your own sipping sound

3. Place the file as `/public/audio/sip.mp3`

The sound is configured to play at 50% volume when the player presses Q to sip coffee.
